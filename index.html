Hello World! My name is Isabel D'Alessandro. I'm a recent graduate of Wellesley College where I studied Neuroscience and Computer Science. Currently, I am working as a research assistant/ computational neuroanatomy technician in the lab of Dr. Rachel Wilson in the Department of Neurobiology at Harvard Medical School.
