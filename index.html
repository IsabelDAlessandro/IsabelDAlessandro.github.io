About Me

Hello World! My name is Isabel D'Alessandro. I'm a junior majoring in Neuroscience and minoring in Computer Science at Wellesley College. I'm particularly intersted in computational and systems neuroscience, and much of the research I've participated in has focused on elucidating how the brain integrates sensory information to create higher-level conscious percepts. I'm also very interested in computational models of intelligence and like to think a lot about both how computers can allow us to gain insight into human brain mechanisms and how the human brain can inspire the creation of artificial intelligences. Feel free to browse this website to check out some of of the projects I've worked on, and research I've participated in.
