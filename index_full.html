I'm Isabel D'Alessandro, a first-year PhD student in the Program in Neuroscience at Harvard. Previously, I worked as a research assistant in the lab of Rachel Wilson at Harvard studying sensory-guided navigation in Drosophila. I'm interested in studying how the brain integrates sensory information to drive complex behaviors.
